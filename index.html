Instructions

To play this game, three seprate segments of drawings must be completed. The order in which they are completed is not important but you cannot complete the same segment twice within the same drawing. You must first select the segment you wish to start with from the prompt dropdrown and then save your drawing once that segment is completed. You will then find the saved drawing in the gallery. From the gallery select the drawing you would like to continue. You will be brought back to the canvas and you can then select the next prompt to contiue the drawing with. Complete all three prompts and the three segements will become a complete and wacky character.
